OPT Calculator

F-1 students may be eligable for a 12-month employment authorization called Optional Practical Training (OPT). OPT opens up an opportunity for students on an F-1 visa to remain in status for an additional twelve months after graduating to gain practical experience in their field of study.

Eligibility for OPT
  • Holding an F-1 visa
  • Be in the final term of study and have applied for graduation
  • Have used less than 12 months of full-time CPT
