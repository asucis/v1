Survey

The CIS International Student Survey is our effort to making the international student exeprience at ASU even better. It's not only about ASU - it's about you and what resources are most important to you as an international students. However, if you do have suggestions to improve the international experience at ASU, please submit them through our feedback form here.

Need more resources as an international student at ASU? Got an idea for improving the experience for international students? Or perhaps you're simply happy with the current experience. Now is the time to let ASU know.

The survey only takes 5-10 minutes to complete. But the input your provide will help shape the experience for international students for the next academic year - and beyond.

You could win Demi Lovato concert tickets. Simply complete and submit the survey by March 3, 2018 and include your ASU email (.asu.edu) and you'll be automatically entered into a drawing to win Demi Lovato concert tickets. Further details of the drawing can be found on the bottom of the page.*

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the International Student Survey

The International Student Survey is a audit that assess the conditions and services being offered to international students at Arizona State University

What will be the survey results be used for?

The data collected in this survey will be analyzed confidentially and anonymously. And on the basis on the results, we can work with university administration to alter or add upon the services ASU currently offers to international students.

We're using a custom Python script which was specifically programmed for this giveaways. It generates a random seed based on the NumPy project. The random pick is the resulting winner.
